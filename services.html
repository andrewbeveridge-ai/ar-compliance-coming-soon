UST / AST Compliance Testing

Most UST violations aren't broken equipment — they're missed testing deadlines. We keep you ahead of both.

Testing requirements vary by system configuration — we'll confirm the exact schedule for your site.
Line & Leak-Detector Testing
What it is
Pressurized piping leak detectors are tested for proper function.
Why it's required
Release detection for pressurized piping under federal 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
Annually for most pressurized systems.
Tank Tightness Testing
What it is
A precision test confirming a tank isn't leaking.
Why it's required
Used for release detection and after a suspected release under 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
As required by your release-detection method or when a release is suspected.
Annual UST Inspections
What it is
A documented walkthrough of the UST system for compliance with operational requirements.
Why it's required
Federal and state operational compliance under 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
Annually.
Release-Detection (ATG) Verification
What it is
Automatic tank gauge operability and release-detection function are confirmed.
Why it's required
Release-detection equipment must be operable under 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
Annually.
Secondary Containment Testing
What it is
Sumps and under-dispenser containment are tested for liquid-tightness.
Why it's required
Containment integrity under 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
Typically every 3 years.
Spill Bucket Testing
What it is
Spill prevention equipment is tested for liquid-tightness.
Why it's required
Spill prevention integrity under 40 CFR Part 280 and Ohio BUSTR (OAC 1301:7-9).
How often
Typically every 3 years.
